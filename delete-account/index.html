Delete Account - Monster World - The Dawn

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Monster World - The Dawn now supports in-app account deletion. This includes guest accounts created through Unity Authentication and accounts linked through Google or Apple. If you can access the Game, use the in-app Delete Account button in Settings first. This page is the fallback path for players who cannot access the Game or whose in-app deletion attempt could not be completed.

Recommended first step: Open the Game, go to Settings > Delete Account, and confirm the deletion there. That is the primary deletion flow and should immediately delete the current in-game account data we control for guest, Google, or Apple sign-in.

Fallback Deletion Request

If you cannot use the in-app deletion flow, complete this form with the account details that match your in-game account. For guest accounts, include your Support ID and player name from the in-game profile if available. This page submits directly to our deletion request inbox for manual review.

Fallback email contact

This static page uses the FormSubmit email form service to deliver requests to psxemerge@gmail.com. Before releasing, submit one test request and complete the email verification from FormSubmit so the form is active.

Important: Uninstalling the app or clearing app data removes local data from your device, but it does not by itself count as an account deletion request. If you played as a guest, use the in-app Delete Account flow before uninstalling whenever possible so the guest account and cloud data are deleted correctly.

What Data We Delete

  • The current Unity Authentication account used by the Game, including guest accounts, when deletion is completed in-app
  • Current Cloud Save data and Cloud Save files controlled by the Game
  • Account-linked game profile information we control
  • Player name and related gameplay progress, currencies, monsters, heroes, items, cosmetics, and other account-linked game data we control

What Data May Be Retained

We may retain limited information when reasonably necessary for the following purposes:

  • Fraud prevention and abuse prevention
  • Security and service integrity
  • Legal compliance, dispute resolution, or enforcement of rights
  • Data retained by third-party platform providers such as Apple or Google under their own policies, including store purchase history and transaction records they control
  • Information needed to verify a fallback deletion request when the player no longer has in-app access, such as Support ID, sign-in method, or contact email

Retention Period

If deletion is completed successfully in-app, the current in-game account data we control is intended to be removed immediately. Fallback web-form requests are reviewed manually, and processing time may vary depending on verification and support workload. In limited cases involving fraud prevention, abuse prevention, security, or legal compliance, certain records may need to be retained longer if required by law.
