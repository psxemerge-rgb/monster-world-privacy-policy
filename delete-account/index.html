This page explains how to request deletion of your Monster World - The Dawn account and associated account-related data controlled by Psxemerge.

Developer / Company Name: Psxemerge
Support email: psxemerge@gmail.com

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to psxemerge@gmail.com.
  2. Use the email subject: Account Deletion Request - Monster World - The Dawn.
  3. Include the following information in your message:
    • SupportID
    • Player Name
    • Sign-in method used (for example: Google, Facebook, or Apple)
Important: Uninstalling the app or clearing app data removes local data from your device, but it does not by itself count as an account deletion request.

What Data We Delete

  • Account-linked game profile information we control
  • Player name and account-related cloud save data we control
  • Game progress, currencies, items, and related account-linked gameplay data that can be removed from our controlled systems

What Data May Be Retained

We may retain limited information when reasonably necessary for the following purposes:

  • Fraud prevention and abuse prevention
  • Security and service integrity
  • Legal compliance, dispute resolution, or enforcement of rights

Retention Period

Deletion requests are reviewed manually. After your request is verified, we aim to complete account deletion and associated data deletion within up to 60 days. In limited cases involving fraud prevention, abuse prevention, security, or legal compliance, certain records may need to be retained for the same period or longer if required by law.

Questions

If you have questions about account deletion or privacy, contact us at psxemerge@gmail.com.
