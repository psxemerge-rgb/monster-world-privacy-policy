This Privacy Policy applies to Monster World - The Dawn, including gameplay, account sign-in, online matchmaking, cloud restore / switch-device features, rewarded ads, and in-app purchases.

D. Multiplayer and matchmaking data

  • Matchmaking metadata

  • Team / participant metadata

E. Purchase and advertising data

  • In-app purchase product identifiers

Payment transactions are processed by the applicable app store or payment platform, not directly by us.
